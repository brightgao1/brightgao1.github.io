EZosuSlider: Easily Add Aesthetic Sliders to Any Beatmap!

Tutorial

1. Choose the .osu file of the map you want to add sliders in w/ File.

2. Copy the time in the editor using Ctrl+c.

3. Click any button/image of the slider you want to add.

4. In the editor, press Ctrl+L and Enter. Do this BEFORE saving the map.

5. Change the slider velocity until the tail is close to where you want it to end, then snap it by slightly moving the last anchor.

You can sort the sliders by length or complexity, ascending or descending. To add sliders to your favorites, right click on a slider an click "Add to Favorites." To go to favorites, Collections->Favorites. On the favorites screen, you can remove from favorites by right clicking and selecting "Remove from Favorites."

Notes

Developed with C/C++ and Win32. EZosuSlider is NOT a virus: Zip scan | Executable scan

The exe is over 11 MB due to the slider images being loaded from resource, not from file. Thus, the image pixel data are embedded into the exe. W/o the images the exe is < 100 KB

Please DO NOT change sliderpoints.txt as if you don't know what you're doing, it can cause beatmaps made w/ EZosuSlider to be corrupted.
